Jewish Education

UJA supports and funds 48 affiliated Jewish day schools, high schools, and supplementary Hebrew schools across the GTA.​

Learn More

Connections to Israel

In Israel, UJA invests in aliyah and absorption, vulnerable populations, education, healthcare, and technology. UJA also forges strong connections between our community and our homeland through a range of locally-run progams.

Learn More

Jewish Identity

UJA inspires the next generation to embrace Jewish life by funding a range of programs and camps that awaken Jewish spirit.​

Learn More

Jewish Poverty Relief

Together with our network of partner agencies, UJA cares for our community’s most vulnerable – those living in poverty, those with mental and physical illnesses or disabilities, new immigrants, seniors, Holocaust survivors – any Jew in need.

Learn More

Jewish Advocacy

UJA supports agencies that advocate for Jews and for Israel, locally and around the globe, to ensure that Jews have a voice and are respected by decision makers, inside and outside of government.

Learn More

Jewish Community

In times of universal celebration or crisis, UJA is the only organization that has the ability to bring together every Jew, regardless of religious, political, or other affiliation.
